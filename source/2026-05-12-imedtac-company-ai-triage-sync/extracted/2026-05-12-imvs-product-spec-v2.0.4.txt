    1. Product Overview
This product is committed to creating a smart health service platform with "self-health management" as the core, responding to the current trend of the three highs and obesity problems becoming increasingly younger, and helping people establish the concept and habit of self-health care. The system integrates individual, community and medical resources, combines the Internet and mobile devices, and realizes regional health care and member health community services.。

The platform also supports cloud-based physiological measurement data analysis and smartphone apps, allowing users to view, record and track their health data at any time, effectively improving health awareness and self-care capabilities. To enhance the user convenience, the system also supports IC card and barcode scanning login function, making the operation more intuitive and smooth, further expanding the public's willingness to use and health participation。

1.2 Product usage situation
In past community health screening activities, people often had to copy down the data themselves after measuring their blood pressure or weight, which was not only prone to errors and omissions, but also not conducive to subsequent tracking. Through the smart biometric measurement station, users only need to log in through IC card or health insurance card to complete multiple measurements such as blood pressure, forehead temperature, weight, height, etc. at one time, and automatically upload the data to the cloud platform or personal health record in real time, greatly improving convenience and data accuracy.

Traditional measurement stations often require staff to assist users, especially when elderly individuals need more time and guidance. The smart biometric measurement station is equipped with voice prompts and a clear, user-friendly interface, making it easy to operate. Even seniors can complete the measurement process independently. This not only reduces the need for caregiver assistance and waiting time but also allows the elderly to monitor their health comfortably and stress-free

In the past, conducting comprehensive health measurements for employees or students at schools or workplaces often required visiting different locations to use various devices like blood pressure monitors and weighing scales. This complicated process reduced users’ willingness to participate. The smart biometric measurement station integrates multiple diagnostic tools into a single device, enabling all measurements to be completed in one place. It is fast, space-saving, and effectively enhances public health awareness and participation.

    1. Support multiple identity verification login methods, such as inserting a health insurance card or entering an ID number, allowing users to operate more flexibly and safely.
